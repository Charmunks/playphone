Signing Up!

Reserve a spot in line here. Once it is your turn to build, you will be pinged in the #playphone channel. You will then have one week to do as much work on the game as you can.

What are the rules?

  1. You must implement something that actually improves the game, no "I changed the player a tiny bit"
  2. No deleting other peoples work without good reason! It's not collaborative if you do everything yourself.
  3. You must make at least 1 commit within the first 3 days of your week, to prevent people from ghosting
  4. You must be 18 or under.
